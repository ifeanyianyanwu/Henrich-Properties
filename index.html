A Home-Grown Success

Henrich Properties Limited is a full-service, privately held commercial and residential real estate investment and development company with a commitment to excellence and tenant service, distinguished resume and reputation established over the years. As a full-service developer, we have the ability to rapidly move any project from site selection, acquisition, financing, construction, leasing, property management and maintenance.
